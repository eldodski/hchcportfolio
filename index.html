About

Design That Lives
Where You Do

Great design is not about following trends. It is about knowing which ones to ignore. I build rooms around the people who live in them, with rich textures, natural wood, confident colour, and not a single piece of furniture that is just for show.

With over eight years of professional experience and hundreds of homeowner consultations, I bring something rare: the ability to manage complex projects, from flooring to fixtures, cabinetry to countertops, without ever losing the personal touch that makes a house feel like yours.

Services

How We Work

◎

Full-Service Design

We provide comprehensive design from concept to completion, including material selections, custom furnishings, contractor coordination, and full project management. We handle every detail so you do not have to.

◊

Room Refresh

This is a targeted transformation for spaces that need a reset. It includes a new furniture plan, updated finishes, curated accessories, and a cohesive design direction, all within a focused scope and timeline.

◦

Design Consultation

This is a focused session to get you unstuck. We will assess your space, define a direction, and give you an actionable plan, whether you are tackling it yourself or ready for full-service support.
