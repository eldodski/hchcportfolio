Section 02

The Problem We Solve

Custom home builders face a recurring pain point: design selections slow down builds. Homeowners get overwhelmed, decisions stall, timelines slip, and the builder absorbs the cost. Outsourcing design to independent firms adds coordination complexity and communication gaps.

ADG eliminates that friction. Our design-to-installation pipeline means selections happen in our Design Center with a professional designer, materials are specified from verified inventory, and installation is scheduled and executed by our own crews. One company. One timeline. No surprises.

Section 04

Services for Builders

Full-Service Design + Installation

The complete package. Ena handles all design selections in our Design Center, specifies materials, and ADG's installation crews execute.

  • Design consultation and needs assessment
  • Full material selections (flooring, tile, countertops, backsplash, cabinetry, fixtures, lighting)
  • Presentation boards / digital design concepts
  • Material specification and ordering
  • Professional flooring installation by ADG crews
  • Project coordination with builder's timeline

Design Selections Only

For builders who have their own installation crews but need professional design guidance for their buyers.

  • Design Center appointments with homeowners
  • Full material selections and specification
  • Presentation boards / digital concepts
  • Material sourcing and pricing

Designer Partnership Program

Independent interior designers can bring their clients to ADG's Design Center for flooring selections and installation. ADG handles the flooring; the designer maintains the client relationship.
