# 1. BRAND FOUNDATION

## 1.1 Who We Are

ADG (United Finishes) is a full-service flooring installation and design company serving custom home builders across San Antonio and the Texas Hill Country. Our Design Center is led by **Ena**, a designer with 8+ years of production-level experience for national builders including **KB Home** and **Pulte Group**.

What sets us apart: builders get a single point of contact for both **interior design selections** and **professional flooring installation**. No middleman. No coordination headaches. One partner from design concept through final install.

## 1.2 The Problem We Solve

Custom home builders face a recurring pain point: design selections slow down builds. Homeowners get overwhelmed, decisions stall, timelines slip, and the builder absorbs the cost. Outsourcing design to independent firms adds coordination complexity and communication gaps.

**ADG eliminates that friction.** Our design-to-installation pipeline means selections happen in our Design Center with a professional designer, materials are specified from verified inventory, and installation is scheduled and executed by our own crews. One company. One timeline. No surprises.

## 1.3 Mission Statement

To streamline the residential design and installation process for custom home builders — delivering design excellence and operational reliability under one roof.

## 1.4 Value Proposition

**For custom home builders who need a design partner that won't slow their builds**, ADG Design Services provides professional interior design selections paired with expert flooring installation — **unlike independent designers who hand off specs and walk away**, ADG owns the process from the Design Center appointment through final installation, keeping builds on schedule and buyers confident.

# 3. SERVICES FOR BUILDERS

## 3.1 Service Packages

### Full-Service Design + Installation
The complete package. Ena handles all design selections in our Design Center, specifies materials, and ADG's installation crews execute.

**Includes:**
- Design consultation and needs assessment
- Full material selections (flooring, tile, countertops, backsplash, cabinetry, fixtures, lighting)
- Presentation boards / digital design concepts
- Material specification and ordering
- Professional flooring installation by ADG crews
- Project coordination with builder's timeline

### Design Selections Only
For builders who have their own installation crews but need professional design guidance for their buyers.

**Includes:**
- Design Center appointments with homeowners
- Full material selections and specification
- Presentation boards / digital concepts
- Material sourcing and pricing

### Design Center Access — Designer Partnership Program
Independent interior designers can bring their clients to ADG's Design Center for flooring selections and installation. ADG handles the flooring; the designer maintains the client relationship.

**How it works:**
1. Designer brings client to ADG for flooring consultation
2. Ena supports the selection process
3. ADG installs — the designer stays focused on their full scope
4. ADG builds relationships with the designer's builder network

**Why designers partner with us:** Access to a professional Design Center without the overhead. Their clients get a better experience. They get reliable installation they don't have to manage.

## 3.2 The Process

| Phase | What Happens | Timeline |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **1. Builder Intake** | Meet with builder to understand the home(s), buyer profile, budget range, and design direction | Day 1 |
| **2. Design Appointment** | Homeowner visits the Design Center. Ena guides full material selections. | Week 1-2 |
| **3. Specification** | Materials specified, pricing confirmed, lead times locked | Week 2 |
| **4. Ordering** | Materials ordered from verified vendors | Week 2-3 |
| **5. Installation** | ADG crews install per builder's construction schedule | Per schedule |
| **6. Walkthrough** | Quality check with builder and/or homeowner | Post-install |

**Average Design Center appointment:** 2-4 hours depending on scope
**Average selection-to-install timeline:** 3-6 weeks (varies by material availability)

## 5.4 Brand Voice — B2B

| Trait | What It Means |
|-------|--------------|
| **Professional** | Business-appropriate. No fluff, no lifestyle language. Speak to timelines, budgets, process. |
| **Confident** | We know what we're doing. State capabilities clearly. |
| **Operational** | Emphasize systems, repeatability, reliability. Builders care about execution. |
| **Partnership-oriented** | "We grow when you grow." Collaborative, not transactional. |

**Do:** Use specific numbers (8+ years, hundreds of homes). Reference builder pain points directly. Speak to ROI and efficiency.

**Don't:** Use consumer-facing language ("transform your space," "dream home"). Don't be flowery. Don't oversell — let the portfolio speak.

# 7. DESIGNER PARTNERSHIP PROGRAM — DETAIL

## 7.1 The Pitch to Independent Designers

> "If you're an independent designer and your clients need flooring, you've got two options: send them to a big-box store and hope for the best, or try to manage the installation yourself. Neither is great. We offer a third option — bring your client to our Design Center, we'll support the flooring selection together, and our crews handle installation. You keep your client relationship. We handle the flooring. And if your builders ever need more, we're here."

## 7.2 How It Feeds the Builder Pipeline

```
Independent Designer → Brings client to ADG for flooring
    → ADG delivers great experience
    → Designer's builder connections see ADG's work
    → Builder inquires about full design + install service
    → ADG wins builder relationship
```

This is a long-game strategy. The immediate value is installation revenue. The real value is builder access through the designer's network.
