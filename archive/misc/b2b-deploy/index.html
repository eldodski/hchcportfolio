Design Leadership

Ena Dodski

I am a design leader with over ten years of experience in high-volume production and semi-custom residential interiors across the Texas Hill Country market. I currently lead the Design Center at United Finishes, where I direct operations for a portfolio of projects ranging from $500K to $1.5M.

Builders partner with me because I keep selections on schedule, presentations professional, and homeowners confident in their choices. I develop scalable design systems that drive upgrade revenue, reduce decision fatigue, and keep builds on timeline.

My approach sits at the intersection of creative vision and operational strategy. I do not just select finishes. I position homes to sell.
